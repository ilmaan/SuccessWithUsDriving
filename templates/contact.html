Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a driving lesson?

You can schedule a driving lesson by filling out our contact form, calling our office at (408) 555-1234, or visiting us in person. We recommend booking at least one week in advance to secure your preferred time slot.

What should I bring to my first lesson?

For your first lesson, please bring your learner's permit or driver's license, comfortable shoes suitable for driving, and any glasses or contacts if you require them for driving. We provide the vehicle for training.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations to avoid charges. We understand emergencies happen, so please contact us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule.

Do you offer pick-up and drop-off services?

Yes, we offer pick-up and drop-off services within a 10-mile radius of our office for an additional fee. This service must be arranged at least 48 hours in advance of your scheduled lesson.
