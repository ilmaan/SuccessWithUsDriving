Instructor Requirements

Driving Instructor

Full-time / Part-time

We're seeking experienced driving instructors to join our growing team. Help students of all ages develop safe driving skills and prepare for their DMV tests.

Requirements:
  • Valid driver's license with clean driving record
  • Minimum 5 years of driving experience
  • Excellent communication and teaching skills
  • Patient, friendly, and professional demeanor
  • Driving instructor certification (or willingness to obtain)
What We Offer:
  • Competitive pay with performance bonuses
  • Flexible scheduling options
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Modern, well-maintained vehicles
  • Supportive and collaborative work environment
