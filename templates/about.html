Our Story

Success with Us Driving School was founded in 2005 by Michael Chen, a former driving instructor with a passion for road safety and driver education. After witnessing too many preventable accidents during his years as a traffic officer, Michael decided to make a difference by creating a driving school focused on safety, confidence, and excellence.

What began as a small operation with just two vehicles has grown into one of Santa Clara's most trusted driving schools, with a fleet of modern training vehicles and a team of professional instructors dedicated to creating the next generation of safe, responsible drivers.

Over the years, we've helped thousands of students from diverse backgrounds achieve their driving goals, from teenagers getting their first license to adults refreshing their skills after years off the road. Our commitment to personalized instruction and road safety has earned us recognition throughout the community.
